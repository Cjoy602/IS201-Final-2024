Education

Voice:

I have received vocal training from Oconee Youth School of Performance for 2 years and took 2 additional years of lessons with a professor at the University Of Georgia. I am also currently enrolled at Brigham Young Universiry with vocal training classes.

Acting/Dance:

I have received both acting and dance training at the Oconee Youth School of Performance as well as training at North Oconee High School. As well as attending multiple acting/musical theater workshops at conventions.

Experience

My acting/singing experience has ranged 12 years, with 18 plus productions of various natures.

Some roles and productions consisting of:
● Newsies -2018 ● The Crucible: Francis Nurse -2018 ● Amadeus: Emperor Joseph II -2019 ● Into The Woods: The Baker -2020

I have participated competitive MT groups with Oconee Youth School of Performance which travel across the state of Georgia area to compete. During High School, I joined my school for annual Georgia Thespian Conferences, which hosted multiple workshops, college auditions, competitive Individual Events, and shows. In all four years of high school, I competed in the school's Literary Team, which takes Quartets, Trios, and other dramatic interpretation events to compete. I also competed annually in One-Act competitions (Which has made consecutive achievements at state).

In recent months, I have participated and served as a “Nauvoo Performing Missionary”. With this, I served under the leadership of my church and performed various dancing, acting, and singing shows everyday for three months. Our rigorous schedule consisted of shows from 10am to 10pm.

About Me

Learn about me and my passions!


I am originally from Athens, Georgia where I was born and raised. I love my home state and all of the beauty that it brings. It was there throughout my childhood and growing-up years that I became exposed to the various outlets of the performing arts and how to express yourself through unique and creative ways.

I started out as an avid lover and audience member watching my siblings perform onstage in performances, which then peaked my own interest. I love how you can take engaging stories from all around the world and turn them into real and authentic stories for other people who come to enjoy.

I think one aspect of the performing arts that appeals to me is the sense of escapism that it provides. It allows its audience to take a step out of reality for a moment and to be encapsulated in worlds of wonder. I feel that there are so many wonderful stories already in the world today, within the lives of ordinary people, that are just waiting to be told. This is what continues to drive me and provide a sense of excitement for this work.
